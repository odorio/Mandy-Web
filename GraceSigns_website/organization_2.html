RELATED ORGANIZATIONS

Access to language is a human right. Here are the links to other organizations that dedicate to help others' language.

Global Down Syndrome Foundation

The Global Down Syndrome Foundation is dedicated to significantly improving the lives of people with Down Syndrome through research, medical care, education and advocacy.

Amazon Smile

AMAZON SMILE If you shop on Amazon, you can easily help out our organization. Next time you visit Amazon, type in Amazon Smile and it will ask you what charity you would like: GraceSigns. It will remember this and whenever you purchase anything, Amazon will make a donation to us.

Positive Exposure

Positive Exposure was founded in 1997 by former fashion photographer Rick Guidotti and Diane McLean and is dedicated to improving the lives of people with genetic differences.

Autism Speaks

The Autism Speaks is a leading organization for autism research and possible cures, increasing awareness, and advocating for the needs of individuals and their families.
